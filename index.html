LEASE AND EARN

LEASE AND EARN is the primary service provided by AGROW to the land or farm owners out there.A land owner can simply list his property for lease only for farming and anyone can take up the lease and reap their own crops and profit out of it.there is no need for human interactions or chaotic paperworks. Every plot is recorded, analysed and approved by prominent professionals and based on this data, we will provide the gain and risk factors for each plot in our list. Each agreement and transaction is kept in a blockchain network.so real world issues like forgery, property valuation and risky money transactions is solved. With the help of AI and Machine Learning, we analyse the data regrading the soil, geographic texture, weather conditions and many other factors affecting the productivity and gives the buyer a clear vision and reduces the risks.
